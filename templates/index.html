The Architecture of Security

01. Complete Data Ownership

Once secured, your encrypted vault is stored directly on your device—not in our cloud. Because we never hold your stored files, a server breach cannot compromise your data.

02. Distributed Key Architecture

We eliminate the risk of stolen passwords. Your access key is mathematically split into 5 shards and routed across segmented edge carriers so no single node can expose your secret.

03. Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Our recovery protocol is entirely blind. When you retrieve your files, our system mathematically verifies your identity and unlocks your vault without our servers ever seeing the contents inside.
